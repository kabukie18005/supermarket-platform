Our Story

Fresh food, real people

We started FreshMart with one simple belief — everyone in Ghana deserves access to fresh, quality groceries at fair prices, delivered right to their door.

Who We Are

Born from a love of fresh food

FreshMart was founded in 2024 with a simple mission: make grocery shopping easier, fresher and more affordable for everyone in Ghana.

We work directly with local farmers and trusted suppliers to bring you the freshest produce every single day. No middlemen, no long supply chains — just fresh food from farm to your door.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown to serve thousands of happy customers across Accra and beyond, and we're just getting started.
