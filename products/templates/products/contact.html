Frequently asked questions

How long does delivery take?
We offer same-day delivery across Accra for orders placed before 2pm. Other areas may take 1-2 business days.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Mobile Money (MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo) and bank cards via our secure Paystack payment gateway.
What if my product is not fresh?
We guarantee 100% freshness. If you receive anything that doesn't meet your expectations, contact us and we'll replace it immediately.
Can I track my order?
Yes! Once your order is placed you can track its status in real time from your My Orders page in your account.
Do you deliver outside Accra?
We currently deliver across Greater Accra. We're expanding to other regions soon — stay tuned for updates!
How do I cancel or modify an order?
Contact us within 1 hour of placing your order and we'll do our best to accommodate any changes or cancellations.
